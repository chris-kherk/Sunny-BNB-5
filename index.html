Importance Of Cleaning & Using Our Own Dedicated Cleaners

Sunny BNB

As a holiday property management business, we understand the importance of maintaining high standards of cleanliness and hygiene in the short term accommodation properties that we manage. One of the ways we ensure this is by using our own cleaners rather than relying on outside cleaning companies.

Read Post

We believe this is important because:

  • Consistency: By using our own cleaners, we can ensure that the cleaning standards are consistent across all properties that we manage. Our cleaners are trained and supervised to follow our cleaning protocols, ensuring that every property is cleaned to the same high standard.
  • Quality Control: Using our own cleaners also allows us to maintain quality control over the cleaning process. We can monitor the cleaners performance and provide feedback and training as necessary to ensure that the cleaning standards are always improving.
  • Flexibility: When we use our own cleaners, we have the flexibility to schedule cleaning appointments at a time that is convenient for guests. This allows us to accommodate guest requests for early check-ins or late check-outs, without compromising on the cleaning standards.

  • Accountability: Our cleaners are accountable to us, and we can hold them responsible for any issues that may arise during the cleaning process. We have a system in place to track the cleaning staff's work, and we follow up on any guest complaints or issues promptly.
  • Security: By using our own cleaners, we can ensure the security of our guests' personal belongings. We have strict procedures in place to safeguard guest property and personal safety of guests and cleaners during the cleaning process.
  • We believe that, using our own cleaners is an essential part of our short term accommodation management services. It allows us to maintain consistent cleaning standards, maintain quality control, provide flexibility, ensure accountability, and enhance guest security. Even the best property will fail if it is not clean and up to a high standard for all guests.
